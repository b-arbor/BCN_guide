Boqueria Market

Personal Favourite

La Boqueria is a famous market located in El Raval with an entrance to the market from La Rambla. It provides a wide variety of fresh fruit, vegetables, nuts and seeds, sweets as well as meat, fish, cheese and dairy. Even if you're not looking to buy anything at the market it is well worth a visit in its own right just to soak up the atmosphere. This place is simply delicious.

Address: La Rambla, 91, 08001 Barcelona, Spain

Blai 9

Tapas

Blai street in Barcelona’s Poble Sec neighborhood is full of pintxos and tapas places. Blai 9 is particularly nice. The food is being served in a form of buffet - you just choose and take what you like as you see it all in front of you. The food is not only delicious, but also beautifully presented.

More info and reviews can be found on the TripAdvisor here

Address: C/ Blai 9, 08004 Barcelona

How to get there: METRO L3 L2 Paral·lel or L3 Poble Sec, BUS 1620, 278, 140

La Xampanyeria

Tapas & Cava

Can Paixano (La Xampanyeria) is a small local cava bar in Port Vell just a short walk from Gothic Quarter (Barri Gotic). This place has amazing local vibe, with tapas and drinks. I recommend trying their cava. At the back of the bar, there's a deli section, so whatever you tried and liked (cava, cheeses, ham, olives, ...) can be also bought in there.

More info and reviews can be found on the TripAdvisor here

Address: Carrer de la Reina Cristina, 7, 08003 Barcelona, Spain
