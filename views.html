The Views

Tibidabo

Tibidabo

Tibidabo is a mountain overlooking Barcelona offering beautiful overview of the city. At the top there’s a temple of the Sacred Heart of Jesus and an amusement park. You will also find an observatory and an ascendable communications tower nearby. Tibidabo can be reached via the Tibidabo Funicular, which was the first of its kind in Spain, and by the Tramvia Blau or road.

Montjuïc Castle

Montjuïc Castle

Montjuïc Castle is an old military fortress, with roots dating back from 1 640, built on top of Montjuïc hill overlooking the city. The castle can be accessed by the Montjuïc cable car, a gondola lift that has its upper station near the castle entrance and connects, via the Montjuïc funicular, with the Barcelona Metro at Paral·lel station.

Bunkers

Personal Favourite

The bunkers de Carmel is a viewpoint from which you can see pretty much the whole city (360º view). I usually just took a bottle of wine or cava up there and watched the sunset. The Carmel Bunkers are located in the upper part of Turó de la Rovira and it is bit of a hike getting up there.
